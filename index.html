We are Lee and Rajo, two life-long Oxfordshire residents running a small-scale mushroom farm located in South Oxford.

We are ideally placed to supply the surrounding community and fine eateries with locally grown mushrooms. Maintaining a low carbon foot print and providing fresh produce that is pesticide free and with minimal food miles. Our mushrooms are grown inside specially modified ex-Ministry Of Defence shipping containers making us Oxfords first mushroom freight farm!
